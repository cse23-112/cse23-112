ABOUT WARNER Bros Entertainment Studios

Warner Bros is an entertainment company that was formed on April 4, 1923, by brothers Harry, Albert, Sam, and Jack Warner.

They started the company as a small film studio in Hollywood, California, which eventually grew into one of the major players
in the entertainment industry. Initially, Warner Bros. focused on producing silent films before transitioning to sound films,
which became a significant breakthrough for the company. Over the years, Warner Bros. has evolved and expanded its operations
to encompass film production, television, animation, and digital media.
